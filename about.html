About

Hi, I'm Mel

I'm a product designer creating intuitive, scalable interfaces for AI-first systems. With a background in architecture, innovation, and business development, I take a structured approach, aligning user needs with business goals. Driven by curiosity and an interest in emerging interaction models, I build products that reduce friction, feel considered in the details, and deliver moments of delight.

When I'm not designing, I'm usually wandering farmers markets, getting my hands and everything else covered in clay, or shuffling a deck for yet another round of Uno. I'm also a big foodie, and always down to swap dinner recommendations.

How I Work

Approach

I approach product design through systems thinking, aligning strategy, UX, and interface craft to deliver coherent experiences across 0-1 products, features, and surfaces.

Methodology

Creative Process

I approach design as an iterative creative practice. I explore ideas through sketches, prototypes, and conversation, using experimentation to refine concepts into clear product directions.

Tools

AI-Native Workflow

AI tools are an integral part of my practice; they let me rapidly explore directions, pressure-test concepts, and prototype interaction flows that can be validated with users and engineering partners.
